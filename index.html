A note from the founder

Hi, my name is Joshua Hwang, and I know what the struggle is like building something as a high school student.

I built a nonprofit called TOTALS when I was a freshman in high school, a free life skill program for kids. We ran three full programs with 20 runs and reached 400 students each year. However, this took many years, and I built from nothing with no connections and no budget. I know what it feels like to send cold emails that go nowhere and to figure out everything alone.

I built Venn because I want to meet other student founders who are going through the same thing. I want to work with you, and I’m here to advocate for the work that you do. It’s impressive that, as a high schooler, you’re putting in the work to change the world through positive nonprofits and your initiatives, and I want to help you.

Venn is always free, and it’s run by someone who has experience and knows what it feels like to run a nonprofit, an organization, or a startup as a high school student and the struggle of it.

Venn One is my promise to you, that I will help you the whole way through.

Joshua Hwang  ·  Founder and CEO, Venn
