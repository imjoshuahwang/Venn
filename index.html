Venn is a free introduction service for student organizations. You're building something real. We find the right people and make the introduction. That's it.

We've connected student orgs to Harvard, Stanford, MIT, Microsoft, and more. Every introduction is personal. Every one is free.

Venn One is our promise: tell us your one goal. We fight for it. If you don't hit it in one week, we run the whole thing again. No charge, ever.

A note from the founder

Hi. My name is Joshua Hwang, and I'm a sophomore in high school in San Jose.

I built a nonprofit called TOTALS when I was a freshman — a free life skills program for kids. We ran three full programs and reached 400 students at the San Jose Public Library. I built it from nothing, with no connections and no budget.

I know what it feels like to send cold emails that go nowhere. To hit every wall. To figure it all out alone.

I built Venn because I kept meeting other student org founders going through the exact same thing. I want to work with you.

Joshua
